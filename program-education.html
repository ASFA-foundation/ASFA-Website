Transforming Lives Through Learning

Our Education Initiative is the cornerstone of ASFA's mission to break the cycle of poverty through education.

We provide comprehensive support including scholarships, school supplies, digital literacy programs, and teacher training to underserved communities across Uganda, Kenya, and Tanzania.
