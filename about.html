About Us

The Arm Stretch Foundation Limited is a company limited by guarantee without share capital.

Who We Are

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of vulnerable children, women, and youth. Our core mission is to empower marginalized communities through education, healthcare, social protection, skills training, and economic empowerment.

Our Objectives

  • To support underprivileged and vulnerable children who are at risk of abuse.
  • To provide access to quality education for needy children and dependants of affected families.
  • To supply basic needs such as clothing and other essentials to ensure children's education is not disrupted.
  • To provide school requirements such as scholastic materials and uniforms to underprivileged children within our operational areas.
  • To acquire, construct, improve, and manage facilities and resources that support education, sports, and talent development for disadvantaged children.
  • To own and operate education facilities, including schools with sports and recreational centers, to promote mental, physical, and creative growth in children.

  • To conduct awareness campaigns aimed at eliminating socio-cultural and gender barriers that hinder the participation of women and the girl child in critical sectors.
  • To train and empower women and youth for leadership and civic engagement, including in areas like agriculture, voting, counseling, and community development.
  • To organize and facilitate sustainable empowerment and financial projects that support women in starting small-scale enterprises such as piggery, poultry, and produce marketing.
  • To promote economic empowerment of women and communities through income-generating initiatives that alleviate poverty in rural areas.

  • To provide medical care to vulnerable children and their families.
  • To educate families—especially women and children—on nutrition and healthy eating habits, ensuring school-going children have access to a balanced diet using locally available foods.
  • To promote hygiene practices among women, youth, and children, including daily bathing, clean clothing, safe water consumption, and food safety.

  • To partner with vocational training centers to equip girls and street youth, including those formerly imprisoned, with practical skills.
  • To construct and maintain vocational training facilities that offer youth employable skills, reducing idleness and promoting self-reliance.
  • To conduct vocational and skills training in areas such as:
    • Tailoring and hairdressing
    • Beauty therapy
    • Carpentry and joinery
    • Art and crafts
    • Motor vehicle mechanics and auto-electricals
    • Electrical installations
    • Welding and fabrication
    • Plumbing
    • Information and communication technologies

  • To protect the rights of children and the elderly through partnerships with organizations and NGOs involved in their welfare.
  • To collaborate with international organizations that focus on women and children, connecting Ugandan women and girls to global initiatives that defend their rights and provide learning opportunities.
  • To provide policy makers and stakeholders with relevant data and insights to address challenges affecting women and girls, and encourage actionable responses.
