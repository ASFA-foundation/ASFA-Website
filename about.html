About Arm Stretch Foundation Africa

Empowering communities since 2025

Who We Are

The Arm Stretch Foundation Africa Ltd is a non-profit organization committed to supporting vulnerable children, women, and youth in Uganda through education, healthcare, empowerment, and social inclusion.

Founded in 2025, we have been at the forefront of community transformation, working hand-in-hand with local stakeholders to create sustainable solutions for the most pressing challenges faced by marginalized populations.

Our Core Principles

Vision

To transform the lives of the hopeless and voiceless into strong, self-reliant individuals.

Mission

Promoting high self-esteem built through hope, love, and care in marginalized communities.

Values

  • P- Promoting hope, love, and care
  • E- Empowering the vulnerable
  • B- Building strong integrity and self-esteem
  • A- Attitude instillation in vulnerable

Our Key Focus Areas

We concentrate our efforts on these critical areas to maximize our impact:

Education & Child Welfare

Ensuring access to quality education and protecting children's rights.

Women & Youth Empowerment

Creating opportunities for economic independence and leadership.

Health & Nutrition Awareness

Improving community health through education and access.

Vocational Skills Development

Equipping individuals with practical skills for sustainable livelihoods.

Advocacy & Partnerships

Collaborating for comprehensive community well-being.

Our Impact in Action

Education Transformation

Providing school supplies and scholarships to underprivileged children in rural communities.

Health Outreach

Organizing medical camps and health education sessions in underserved areas.

Skills Training

Empowering women and youth with vocational skills for economic independence.
