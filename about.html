About Arm Stretch Foundation Africa Ltd

Empowering communities since 2025

Who We Are

The Arm Stretch Foundation Africa Ltd is a non-profit organization committed to supporting vulnerable children, women, and youth in Uganda through education, healthcare, empowerment, and social inclusion.


Vision

To transform the lives of the hopeless and voiceless into strong, self-reliant individuals.


Mission

Promoting high self-esteem built through hope, love, and care in marginalized communities.


Values

  • P- Promoting hope, love, and care
  • E- Empowering the vulnerable
  • B- Building strong integrity and self-esteem
  • A- Attitude instillation in vulnerable

Key Focus Areas

  • Education & Child Welfare
  • Women & Youth Empowerment
  • Health & Nutrition Awareness
  • Vocational Skills Development
  • Advocacy & Partnerships for Community Well-being
