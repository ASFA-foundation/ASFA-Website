Arm Stretch Foundation Africa Ltd (ASFA)

Arm Stretch Foundation Africa Ltd (ASFA) is a legally registered non-profit organization with registration number 80020003800857. Founded by Ategyeka Alvin, ASFA works with vulnerable children, orphans, people with disabilities, and marginalized groups including street kids, single mothers, teenage mothers, women, youth, elderly persons, and people living with HIV/AIDS.

Located in Fort Portal Tourism City, Western Uganda, ASFA is dedicated to providing hope, love, and care to the voiceless and hopeless in Uganda. The organization was founded after understanding the daily challenges faced by underprivileged individuals and the desire to improve their lives.

ASFA believes that restoring hope and dignity to vulnerable people is key to a better society and development. Our work focuses on nurturing talents, skills, and encouraging self-employment among communities. Through our initiatives, we aim to help vulnerable children, women, and youth gain confidence, self-esteem, and independence.

VISION

To turn the hopeless and voiceless into strong, hopeful, and self-reliant individuals for a better tomorrow.

MISSION

To promote high self-esteem built through hope, love, and care in communities of vulnerable and marginalized people.

CORE VALUES

  • P - Promoting hope, love, and care
  • E - Empowering the vulnerable
  • B - Building integrity and self-esteem
  • A - Attitude instillation

OBJECTIVES

  • To provide basic needs to vulnerable children, street kids, and orphans
  • To empower single mothers, teenage mothers, and women with entrepreneurial skills
  • To support people living with HIV/AIDS with counseling and economic assistance
  • To promote inclusive education and reduce stigma for persons with disabilities
  • To engage youth in skill development and talent nurturing activities
  • To advocate for the rights and dignity of marginalized communities
  • To create sustainable programs that uplift the livelihoods of underprivileged individuals
