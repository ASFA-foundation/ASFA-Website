About Us

Our Mission & Vision

Arm Stretch Foundation Africa is committed to empowering vulnerable communities across Africa through sustainable development initiatives in education, healthcare, and shelter.

Our vision is to create a continent where every individual has access to basic human rights and opportunities for growth and development.

Since our establishment, we have impacted over 15,000 lives across 8 African countries, building schools, healthcare facilities, and homes for those in need.
